Writing

A dump of writing I've accumulated over the years, edited to fit in a blog format.

2025

  • Autumn 2025 paper
    A Fair Coin and Conservativeness

    On whether probabilistic science threatens Field's conservativeness requirement, and what the fair-coin case reveals about the limits of a purely nominalist base.

  • Summer 2025 paper
    Competition Without Transparency

    The US biosimilar market fails to deliver competition's promised savings not because of a lack of competitors, but because payer incentives systematically favor high-list-price drugs over genuinely cheaper ones.

  • Summer 2025 essay
    The Wrong Contract

    Value-based payment contracts for CAR-T therapy are designed between payers and manufacturers, but the binding access barrier sits with hospitals, which lose money on every Medicare inpatient case under a DRG system built for a different era of medicine.

  • Summer 2025 essay
    The Agent Problem

    PBM-insurer vertical integration resolves the common agency inefficiency that kept drug costs high, but replaces it with a foreclosure dynamic and a structural mechanism for circumventing the Medical Loss Ratio constraint that conduct-based regulation is poorly designed to address.

  • Spring 2025 paper
    An Exploration of Hypercomputation

    Exploring methods to break the Church-Turing Thesis
